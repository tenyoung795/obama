Super Obama Bros.

Controls (both players)

Up, down, left, right (move, duck, climb): Directional keys
A button (jump, swim): X
B button (run, shoot): Z
Start (pause): Enter
Select: Shift
Refresh to restart

Plot

Al-Qaeda has kidnapped First Lady Michelle Obama and the seven SWAT members sent for her. It's up to the President and Vice President Joe Biden to save the SWAT team, rescue the First Lady, and defeat Osama bin Laden!

Gameplay

Gameplay is identical to the original Super Mario Bros., except

  • The player is always big.
  • The player's health starts at two health points and goes up to five.
  • There is no Fire Flower.
  • Hammers are now bullets.
  • At the end of each castle, the player is equipped with a gun to defeat Bin Laden.
  • The axe by Bin Laden does not work until the player defeats Bin Laden.
  • Bin Laden's health increaes per world.
